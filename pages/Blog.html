ABOUT TECHNOLOGY

Published on: jan 12,2024

Technology. We all know what Technology is. Every day we use something to make our thing easier and the medium used to make it easier is Technology. We are living in the world full of technology. Technology is all about human creation. Technology is the thought, creation of human being that is implemented on a hardware software tools. Technology is just making the life of human being easier, reliable. The more advanced technology the more feature it provide. Its just changing the human environment slowly.

POSITIVE IMPACT OF TECHNOLOGY

Every morning from opening our eye in morning to closing our eye in the night, we need technology. Once we open the eyes in the morning everything starts with technology like alarm clock makes us awake. Technology has not made our life easier instead it has been an essential things in our lives. It has become a solution for every problem on our lives like when we like to study, we can study it from our device rather than opening our textbook. Technology has made human to remember a time when one has to waits for a month for receiving letter to just clicking the button to communicate. Technology has made easier to communicate with the relatives , parents and with our close ones via various means like WhatsApp, Instagram, messenger and made the world into a global village. Technology has made a great positive impact in long distance relation by connecting the two people around the corner of the world via video conferencing, communication and feeling them like a real time togetherness. Technology has created such a platform that school and college can run their classes by virtually. Students can access any kind of study materials from the internet. Student do not have to depend only on the teacher rather can learn anything from the internet. Various research has proved technology has helped the students in various ways. They make the students more competitive.

NEGATIVE IMPACT OF TECHNOLOGY

Everything has its two side likewise; technology too gives positive impacts and negative impacts. Increasing advancement in technology has many negative impacts in human life and also the environment. Technology has connected people via virtually which has created a great distance in a family. Overtime and excessive use of technology leads to different health problem. Advancement of technology has made the things serious like students depend on the technology and here the creativity level of students decrease. Excessive use of technology leads to addiction and also consume the productive time. With the rise in use of technology in all age group are slowing declining face to face interaction. Overuse of technology has issued in the sleeping routine. Many teenage uses their device whole night, which to leads many problems like lack of concentration in the class. Using of technology regularly without any time management, leads to poor body posture. It mainly hampers our eyesight because mobile, laptops and other device emit such a light which directly hampers the human brain. Growing children are more sensitive in all aspects from negative to positive things, and the use of technology in these children brings many bad effects in them like losing social connection, loosing concentration, aggressive behaviour, addiction of technology, poor level of creativity.

IMPACT OF TECHNOLOGY ON DAILY LIFE AND BUSINESS / PRINTING BUSINESS

With technology there is an increased scope for financial stability. Technology helped business to increase the productivity by allowing to access data which helps in decision making process. Cloud computing which is a great part of technology gives to access the data from any device or also from any location. With rapidly advancement of technology, business nowadays offer a great amount of products and service to a customer via their device. In printing business, technology uses as laser and inkjet which helps to give output quicky as compared to traditional method.

CONCLUSION

Business and human should always use the technology in a advantageous way to stay competitive in the global market. Technology has made a greater way to have a easier life for human in all sector from transportation to health sector and many more.
